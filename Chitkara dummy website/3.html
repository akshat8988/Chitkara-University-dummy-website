CHITKARA SCHOOL OF HEALTH SCIENCE

Chitkara College of Applied Engineering has been established in Chitkara University to crystallise the ‘Make in India’ mission of Hon’ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi and to provide opportunities of skill development along with vertical mobility leading to gainful employment of diploma students joining Engineering Programs.

Chitkara College of Applied Engineering is a Chitkara Education initiative to provide students, opportunity to grow vertically & horizontally in their career paths with Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, and Safety & Fire specialisations. All our programs come with mandatory industry internship and are offered with 100% placement assistance in our partner organisations, which facilitate a successful career start in the chosen industry domain.

On-the-job training is an integral part of our Engineering degree & diploma programs at Chitkara College of Applied Engineering, and are meant to not only give the students a real-life exposure of the Industry, but also allow the employer to evaluate the students, as they work on the tasks given to them. The academic approach for diploma & degree students is maximum industry orientation/ exposure with great stress on practical skills enhancement.
